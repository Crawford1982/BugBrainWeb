Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

We keep this simple because we're developers too — not data hoarders.

TL;DR

We don't track you beyond what's necessary to keep the site running. We're not here to spy, sell, or store creepy amounts of info.

What We Collect

  • Scan Input: When you use tools like IDORx, we process your input (e.g. URLs) to run the scan — but we don't log or store them unless you're on a pro plan that enables history.
  • Basic Analytics: We use Plausible.io to understand general usage. It's privacy-first, cookie-free, and doesn't fingerprint.
  • Email (Optional): If you join a waitlist or sign up for early access, we'll store your email securely via our email provider. No spam. Ever.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't track you across the internet.
  • We don't log your scans (unless you ask us to in Pro mode).

Got Questions?

Ping us at hello@bugbrain.dev — a human reads every message.
